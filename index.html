Pomodoro Timer App

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method that involves breaking work into intervals called "pomodoros".

Here are the steps:
  • Choose a task.
  • Set a timer for 25 minutes (one Pomodoro).
  • Work on the task with full concentration.
  • Take a 5-minute break after each Pomodoro.
  • After completing four Pomodoros, take a longer break of 15-30 minutes.
